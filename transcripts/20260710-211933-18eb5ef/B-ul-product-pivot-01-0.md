### Recommendation
**Start with A (validation) but keep it lean.**

Why: Productizing without customer signals is how you end up with features no one wants. Five customer conversations take 2–3 weeks and will tell you more than any internal analysis. You'll learn:
- Whether this is a "nice to have" or a "solve my biggest problem" for potential buyers
- What they'd actually pay
- What's table-stakes vs. nice-to-have
- Whether you're talking to the same personas or if different segments want different things

You can interview people while the tools are still rough. The question you're asking isn't "is the product perfect?" — it's "is there a buyer?"
