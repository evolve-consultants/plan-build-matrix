### Why this approach
- **Confident framing**: leads with the ship, not the ask. States the problem plainly rather than overselling the solution.
- **Specificity**: names the actual friction (leaving the platform), so it lands for people who feel it and doesn't feel generic.
- **Proof by doing**: "we've been using it internally" is more credible than hypothetical benefits.
- **Minimal CTA**: assumes the audience can self-direct; doesn't push.
- **Structure**: problem → solution → evidence → access → support. Moves fast.
