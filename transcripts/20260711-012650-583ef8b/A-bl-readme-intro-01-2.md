**Perfect for:** [Type of user] who want to [goal] quickly.

## Why you might want this

- **[Benefit 1]** — [one sentence explanation]
- **[Benefit 2]** — [one sentence explanation]
- **[Benefit 3]** — [one sentence explanation]

## Quick start (2 minutes)

```bash
[Your fastest install command]
```

Then do [the one essential thing]. Done.

---

## Tips for your version:

- **Lead with the *why***, not the *what*. Users care about their problem first.
- Use a **relatable analogy** in the opening — it bridges the gap instantly
- **One emoji** per section (not overdone) adds visual breathing room
- Keep each bullet to **one punchline** — expand elsewhere
- The "Quick start" should be literally 2-3 lines, not 2-3 paragraphs

Once you fill in the specifics, you'll have something that hooks people in 10 seconds instead of losing them in paragraph one.
