# Subject: We've launched booking requests

Hi everyone,

We're launching booking requests today.

Here's what it does: customers can now request a time slot directly from your availability calendar. You get a notification, review the details, and confirm or suggest alternatives—all without the back-and-forth emails.

**Why this matters:**
- Customers get faster responses (no waiting for you to check your inbox and reply)
- You control your availability and can batch confirmations
- Everything stays in one place instead of scattered across email threads

**How to get started:**
1. Go to Settings > Booking
2. Set your availability window (next 30 days, 60 days, custom)
3. Share your booking link with customers

You can turn it on just for certain services or customer segments—test it out however makes sense for your workflow.
