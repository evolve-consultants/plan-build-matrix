# Dashboard Fix: Problem-Solving Framework

I'd need more specifics to actually help, but here's how to diagnose and fix it:

## Quick Diagnostics

**Ask your users directly:**
- What are they trying to do that feels hard?
- What confuses them most? (layout, terminology, finding data, understanding metrics)
- Where do they get stuck or give up?

**Check your data:**
- Analytics: Which pages have high bounce rates or low engagement?
- Support tickets: Common complaint patterns?
- User testing: Watch 3-5 people use it without help

## Common Dashboard Problems & Fixes

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Too much info | Remove non-essential metrics; add filtering/views |
| Unclear labels | Use plain language; add tooltips explaining metrics |
| Poor hierarchy | Highlight what matters most; group related items |
| Slow to load | Optimize queries; lazy-load sections |
| Can't find things | Add search, clear navigation, logical grouping |
