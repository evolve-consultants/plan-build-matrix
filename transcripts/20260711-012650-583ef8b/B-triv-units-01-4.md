**Trivial — matrix not applied.** Classified this as a factual lookup because there's a single, unambiguous definition with no downstream decisions depending on the answer.

1 gibibyte (GiB) = 1,024 mebibytes (MiB).

(Mebibytes are 2^20 bytes; gibibytes are 2^30 bytes. So 2^30 ÷ 2^20 = 2^10 = 1,024.)
