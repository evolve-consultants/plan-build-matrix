# Design System Talk Abstract Template

Here are a few approaches depending on your angle:

## Problem-First (Most Engaging)
**"[Team/Company] Built a Design System. Here's What We Got Wrong."**

We scaled from X to Y products without shared standards—until the cost of inconsistency forced our hand. This talk covers the three biggest missteps we made (and how we corrected them), what we'd do differently, and the one decision that actually moved the needle.

## Journey-Based (Human Interest)
**"From Chaos to Scale: How We Built a Design System That Stuck"**

Design systems fail when they're imposed. We built ours by listening to the teams rejecting it first. I'll walk through our phased approach, the unexpected resistance we hit, and how we turned skeptics into advocates—without forcing adoption.

## Concrete-Outcome (Results-Driven)
**"[Metric]: What Changed When We Unified Design"**

When we launched our design system, X happened. This talk isn't about *how* we built it—it's about *why it mattered*. I'll show the before/after, the three leverage points that moved the needle, and a framework you can use to measure success at your organization.

---

**What's your actual story?** Once I know the core insight (what surprised you? what worked? what failed?), I can sharpen the angle further.
