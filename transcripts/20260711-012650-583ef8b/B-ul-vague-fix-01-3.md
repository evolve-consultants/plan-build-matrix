### Typical dashboard confusion patterns (to help you answer)
- **Information overload**: too many metrics, charts, or options visible at once
- **Unclear information hierarchy**: users don't know where to look first
- **Poor labeling**: jargon, ambiguous names, or missing context
- **Hidden features**: users don't know something exists
- **Broken workflows**: the steps to accomplish a task are illogical or buried
- **Inconsistent mental models**: layout doesn't match how users think about the domain
- **Visual clutter**: too many colors, borders, or competing elements
