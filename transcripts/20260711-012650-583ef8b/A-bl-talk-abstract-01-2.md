# Design System Origin Story

**The Talk:** How We Built Our Design System Without Losing Our Minds

What starts as "let's standardize our buttons" often becomes a months-long project with competing priorities, skeptical teams, and the nagging question: *Are we solving a real problem?*

This talk walks through the messy middle of building a design system from scratch—the decisions that mattered, the ones we overthought, and the ones we got wrong the first time. You'll hear about our early mistakes (spoiler: we built too much), how we got buy-in from teams who'd rather move fast, and the metrics that actually proved ROI.

Whether you're starting from chaos or refining what you have, this is a story about shipping something useful instead of perfect.

**What you'll take away:**
- The framework we used to decide what goes in vs. what stays out
- How we got developers, designers, and product on the same page
- The one thing we'd do differently
