### Recommendation
**Start with A (Validation)**, but do it *operationally lightweight*.

Here's why: You already have paying customers (your consultancy clients). Before you build a product business, confirm that the value you're seeing internally actually translates to external customers who would *pay for it separately*. This takes 4–8 weeks, costs almost nothing, and either unlocks confidence for a real launch or saves you from a year of misdirected effort.

Validation doesn't mean surveys or focus groups. It means:
1. **Identify who else has this problem** — beyond your own industry if possible
2. **Talk to 5–10 of them** about how they solve it now (what they pay, what they hate)
3. **Show them your tool** — even rough — and gauge genuine interest
4. **Test willingness to pay** — "If this were $X/month, would you use it?" gets more honest answers than "would you want this?"

If validation is strong, you move to MVP with confidence. If it's weak, you've learned something valuable without sunk cost.
