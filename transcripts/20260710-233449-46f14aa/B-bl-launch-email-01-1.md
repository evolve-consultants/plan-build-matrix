### Why this structure

- **Opens with the fact**: "It's live" anchors everything that follows.
- **Second paragraph is pure utility**: three concrete things the feature does, ordered by user benefit.
- **Third paragraph shows integration**: context matters only if it clarifies what they already care about.
- **Reasoning, not pitch**: explains *why* this exists without selling.
- **Closes with action**: clear next step, no urgency or scarcity language.
- **Voice**: declarative, direct, assumes competence.
