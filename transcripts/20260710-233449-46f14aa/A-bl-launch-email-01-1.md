# Subject: Booking is now live

We've launched booking.

If you manage appointments, consultations, or any kind of one-on-one time with clients, you can now offer a booking link instead of the back-and-forth emails. Clients pick their slot, confirm their details, and you get a calendar invite—no manual scheduling required.

**What it does:**
- Custom booking pages that match your brand
- Automatic timezone handling
- Prep questions before each appointment
- Calendar sync (Google, Outlook, iCal)
- Reminder emails so no-shows drop off

**Who it's for:**
Coaches, consultants, therapists, photographers, freelancers—anyone whose time is their product. If you're currently using Calendly or Acuity, you can import your settings and move over.

It integrates with everything you're already using. Your workflows don't change. You're just removing the friction.

You can turn it on in Settings > Booking, or [read the docs](link) if you want the full breakdown.

Thanks for being patient while we built this. We tested it with 200+ of your accounts over the last three months. The feedback shaped what shipped.
