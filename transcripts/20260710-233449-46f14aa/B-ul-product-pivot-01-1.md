### Recommendation

**Start with A (Validate-first)**, but with a specific structure:

1. **List 20 potential customer archetypes** — not just "companies like yours," but specific roles/departments/company sizes that would care about the problem you solve.
2. **Run 15 structured conversations** (not sales pitches; discovery calls). Ask:
   - What's your current solution to this problem?
   - How much time/money does it cost you?
   - Would you switch if something better existed? At what price point?
   - Who else in your org would need to buy in?
3. **Measure: Do at least 3–5 people express serious interest and willingness to be early customers?** (Not "that's cool," but "I'd use that if…")
4. **Assess extraction effort**: Can you pull the tool out of your consultancy's context in a reasonable time?

If validation is clear, you'll have evidence to either commit to B or C next. If it's ambiguous, you've saved yourself months of wasted effort.
