**1,024 mebibytes (MiB) = 1 gibibyte (GiB).**

This follows the binary progression: 1 GiB = 2^30 bytes, and 1 MiB = 2^20 bytes, so 2^30 ÷ 2^20 = 2^10 = 1,024.
