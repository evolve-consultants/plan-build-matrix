| **A: Diagnostic first** | Gather specific user feedback (interviews, analytics, heatmaps, task recording) before redesigning. Identify the actual pain points. | You don't have clear data on *what's* confusing or you suspect the problem is different than it seems. |

### Recommendation
**Start with Option D (Hybrid).** Here's why:

1. **"Confusing" is vague.** You could fix the wrong thing and waste effort.
2. **Diagnosis is fast.** A few user interviews + quick analytics review takes 3-5 days and gives you concrete data.
3. **Avoids big redesigns.** Once you know what's actually broken, you probably don't need to rebuild everything.
4. **Builds confidence.** When you do redesign, you know it'll work because you're solving a real problem users named.
