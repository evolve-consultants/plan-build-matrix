### Recommendation
**Start with A (Diagnostic).** 

Confusion is user-specific. What confuses a new user might be perfect for a power user, and vice versa. Without knowing *which* users and *what tasks*, any fix is a guess. 

Five minutes of structured user questions (or a review of your existing feedback) will tell you whether the problem is:
- Information discovery ("I can't find what I need")
- Terminology ("I don't understand what this means")
- Workflows ("I don't know what to do next")
- Visual design ("Too much/too little/wrong emphasis")
- Something else

Once we know, the fix becomes obvious.
