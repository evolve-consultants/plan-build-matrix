# Quick diagnostic first

Before jumping to fixes, I need to understand the actual problems:

**Ask your users:**
- What are they trying to do that feels confusing?
- Where do they get stuck?
- What do they expect to find but can't?

**Check your data:**
- Are there support tickets/feedback mentioning specific pain points?
- Do analytics show where users drop off?
- Are certain features unused?

---

# Common dashboard fixes (likely culprits)

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| **Too much information** | Remove/hide non-essential elements; progressive disclosure |
| **Poor hierarchy** | Make important things bigger/higher; reduce visual noise |
| **Unclear navigation** | Label clearly; consistent placement; breadcrumbs |
| **Confusing data visualization** | Better labels, legends, color choices; simpler charts |
| **No clear entry point** | Guide new users; obvious primary actions |
| **Outdated/broken features** | Remove them or fix them |

2. **Note where they pause/click wrong places**
3. **Ask "what is this?" about confusing elements**
4. **Prioritize fixes** by frequency of confusion
5. **A/B test** changes before full rollout
