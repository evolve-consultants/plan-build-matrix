# 3-Email Onboarding Sequence

## Email 1: Welcome + Quick Win (Send immediately)
**Subject:** Your scheduling app is ready—start here

- Warm welcome
- One specific thing to do in next 10 min (connect calendar OR create first event)
- Screenshot or short GIF showing where to find it
- Keep copy to ~150 words
- CTA: "Complete your first sync"
- Link to help doc if they get stuck

---

## Email 2: Show the Value (Day 2-3)
**Subject:** [Name], here's what just got easier

- Show them data from their trial: "You've added X events" or "Your calendars are synced"
- Highlight ONE feature that solves their biggest scheduling pain (conflict detection? resource booking? team visibility?)
- Short success story or stat ("Teams using this save 4 hours/week on scheduling")
- CTA: "Explore [feature name]"
- Link to a walkthrough or tutorial video

---

## Email 3: Last Nudge Before Expiration (Day 12-13 of 14-day trial)
**Subject:** Don't lose your setup—upgrade before [date]

- Recap what they've built/configured
- Specific pricing/plan that fits their use case
- Social proof: testimonial or case study
- Remove friction: mention onboarding support, trial extension if needed
- CTA: "Upgrade now" + secondary CTA: "Chat with us first"

---

**What to adjust:** 
- Where's the biggest friction in *your* onboarding? 
- What do users who convert actually use first?
- Any trial limits/blockers we should address earlier?
