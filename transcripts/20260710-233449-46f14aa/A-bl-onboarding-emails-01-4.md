# Trial Onboarding Sequence

## Email 1: Welcome + Quick Win (Send immediately)
**Subject:** Your scheduling app is ready — 2-min setup

- Brief welcome, genuine tone
- **One thing to do first:** Create their first event (make it dead simple)
- Screenshot or short GIF of the core action
- Reassure them the free trial includes [X days]
- Link to support if stuck
- Avoid: Feature dump, multiple CTAs

**Tone note:** Helpful, not salesy. Act like you're helping a friend, not closing a deal.

---

## Email 2: "Here's what people do with this" (Day 3–4)
**Subject:** Line 1: [First name], here's what's working for other teams
or
**Subject:** The thing most people miss (and how to avoid it)

- Brief story or pattern you've noticed ("Teams that X see Y results")
- Show 2–3 realistic use cases relevant to *their signup type* (if you segment)
- One feature highlight that connects to a real problem
- Soft CTA: "See it in action → [link to feature or guide]"
- End with: "Questions? Reply to this email"

**Tone note:** You're a knowledgeable peer, not a tour guide.

---

## Email 3: Before Trial Ends (Day 10–11, adjust to your timeline)
**Subject:** Your trial ends in [X days] — here's what we'd hate you to miss

- Acknowledge if they've used it; reference their actual behavior if possible ("I see you created 12 events")
- Surface one thing they *haven't* tried yet that matches their use case
- Address friction/objection indirectly: "Most people ask about [common question]"
- Clear CTA: Convert to paid, or ask a clarifying question ("What would help you decide?")
- Option to extend trial if appropriate

**Tone note:** Respectful, not pushy. "We'd love to keep working with you, but no pressure."

---

## Notes to shape:
- **Timing:** Adjust based on your trial length and user behavior data
- **Segmentation:** Do you know *why* they signed up? (Team scheduling vs. personal vs. class scheduling?) Customize email 2 accordingly
- **Behavior triggers:** If you have analytics, skip email 2 for power users, or send a different version
- **Support integration:** Make sure replies actually go somewhere and get answered within 24h
